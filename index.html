Loaded Fries

Toss short ribs with flour and pepper, shaking off excess; reserve remaining flour mixture. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; brown ribs on all sides. Transfer to a 3-qt. slow cooker, reserving drippings. Add garlic; cook and stir 1 minute. Stir in 1 cup stock; bring to a boil, stirring to loosen browned bits from pan.In a small bowl, whisk reserved flour mixture, chili sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar and remaining stock until smooth; stir into onion mixture. Pour over ribs.Cook, covered, on low until ribs are tender, 6-8 hours. Remove ribs; shred with 2 forks and keep warm. Serve beef over potatoes; top with gravy and cheese curds.

Healthy Oatmeal

In a small saucepan, add all ingredients 2 cups unsweetened almond or coconut milk, 1 cup hemp seeds,4 tbsp ground flaxseed, 2 tbsp chia seeds, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 2 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 cup almond flour, 2 tbsp coconut oil, 1/2 cup organic berries of choice (Optional except the almond flour. Stir until well combined, then heat over medium heat until you bring the mixture to a light boil. Cook for another 1-2 minutes.Remove from heat and stir in almond flour.Top with berries if desired and serve.With so many impressive benefits of flaxseeds, we strongly encourage you to keep them as a regulate part of your diet, add them to smoothies, baked goods,make our highly nutritious keto hemp seed porridge.

Hot garlic noodles

Firstly, in a large vessel take 6 cup water. Once the water comes to a boil place a pack of hakka noodles (125 gm) boil the noodles for 3 minutes or till it turns. Pour 1 cup of cold water to prevent from further cooking. Rizzle 1 tsp oil and mix gently, to avoid noodles from sticking to each other. keep aside. Now in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and 1 chilli.aute ½ onion, 2 tbsp spring onion on high flame. Further add ½ carrot, ¼ green capsicum, 5 snow pea, 5 beans, chopped, ¼ red capsicum and 5 florets broccoli. Stir fry until sauces are well combined. Toss and mix until noodles are cooked completely. Finally, garnish with spring onion and enjoy veg noodles.

Smoky mac 'n' cheese pasta

In a pan, heat butter. Let it melt. Add maida, keep the flame low-to-medium. Add milk in batches, keep stirring gradually so that no lumps are formed. Repeat till you add all milk and get a smooth sauce. Add salt, black pepper powder, nutmeg powder, oregano, red chilli flakes. Throw in the grated cheese. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add cream, mix well. This adds more richness to your pasta. Add boiled pasta, and grate more cheese on top. Mix everything well, make sure there is no lump formation at any stage. Your pasta is done. Serve hot with a sprinkling of oregano.You can also add more elements to your pasta, veggies like broccoli, bell peppers add a nice crunch, mushrooms lend it a beautiful texture too.

Classic papperoni Pizza

Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take warm water, sugar and dry yeast and give a quick mix allow it to rest for 5 minutes. Now add maida, salt and olive oil. Knead the dough adding water as required. Tuck the dough and form to a ball. place in a large mixing bowl. Cover rest and rise in a warm place for about 2 hours. Punch the dough with fist to release down the air.place the dough on dusted pizza plate or tray. Then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork. Furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly. Then spread grated cheese and sprinkle italian seasonings. Further, bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit for about 12 - 15 minutes. Finally, slice and serve pizza hot.
